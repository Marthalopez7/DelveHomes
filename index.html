The Machine

The Machine has perfect cozy sized homes. The new honeycomb designs were made to be compact and comfortable even while underground. The best part is that these homes come equipped with dozens of accommodating buttons, which make up for the small spacing. Examples of such are buttons for a bath, food, and music. Use of buttons will vary depending on the resident, but manuals will always be provided. There is also great service installed for long distance communication. Residents will not have to leave into the outside world to communicate with others. It's all provided at the click of a button. No physical labor is needed either since those magnificent buttons can do anything with a press. Previous residents have stated The Machine honeycombs are such an enjoyable experience they forgot they were underground. Everything they needed for living was provided with buttons. Replacements for things such as medical checkups or physical strength are included. Each home has its own set of top tier medication so residents may stay in the comfort of their own homes. These homes may not be for everyone, but they are incredible forms of advancement. There is no need for personal skills or talents, the advanced technology will make everything easier to live in. This home truly has it all!
