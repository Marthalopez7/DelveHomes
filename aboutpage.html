About Us

Get to know the history behind the machine

The machine was built a long time ago by three different creators. Andrea Picquadio, the main engineer, Christina Morillo the main founder, and Italo Melo the UX designer. They are the creators of the machine and everything that goes along with it. Since their passing, different people have each learned one part of the machine and specialize in the section. They are the ones that keep the machine in its most pristine condition. Thus, keeping the machine fit for many future generations to use.

The main founder, Christina Morillo, created the idea of the machine and set the whole project in motion. With her vision of the machine in mind, she reached out to her old friends Andrea Piacquadio and Italo Melo, to help her complete this project. With all of their talented skills and backgrounds, they managed to create the machine.

The main engineer, Andrea Piacquadio, has the consistent mindset of innovating. She took care of the design, build, and maintenance of the various complex structures.
